Intraday signals — HFT scanner

Building blocks of a 0–1 DTE setup, detected on 5-minute bars, each scored 0–100.

VWAP
Volume-Weighted Average Price — the intraday "fair value" line. Price above = bullish bias, below = bearish.
VWAP reclaim
Price crosses back above VWAP on rising volume after being below it — a bullish momentum trigger.
VWAP bounce
In an uptrend, price pulls back to VWAP, holds it as support, and turns up — a continuation entry.
ORB
Opening Range Breakout — price breaks the high/low of the session's opening range.
Volume spike
A bar trading at a large multiple of recent average volume — real participation behind a move.
Range breakout
Price breaks the high/low of the last ~12 bars (≈1h) on volume — a fresh directional push.
Momentum burst
A short-term surge (fast RSI/ROC) confirming acceleration. Scores but doesn't count toward the confluence floor (noisy alone).
Strong bar
Price-action filter: the bar closes in the top/bottom ~30% of its range — buyers/sellers won the bar.
Trend alignment
The setup's direction agrees with the broader intraday trend (a filter, not a standalone trigger).
Prime session
The mid-session window (≈9:45 AM–2:45 PM ET) the bot trades — avoids the noisy open and EOD close.

How signals combine

Composite score
Weighted blend of the signals above (0–100); proven signals (VWAP/ORB/spike) weigh most. Must clear the minimum score.
Confluence
How many distinct core signals fire at once. Floor of 3 — single/double-signal setups historically lost money.
Conviction
High = the proven VWAP+ORB+spike trio all fire (full size). Relaxed = another qualifying combo (half size).

Post-earnings / news drift — PEAD scanner

Trades the continuation of a big news-driven move, after the gap.

Drift
The tendency of a stock to keep moving in the direction of a large catalyst for days afterward.
Event
A qualifying catalyst day: an abnormal daily return (≥ z-score threshold) + a minimum gap % + heavy volume.
Gap
The overnight jump in price from the catalyst.
Volume multiple
How many times normal volume the event traded — confirms it's real, not noise.
Held fraction
How much of the move has stuck since it happened — only drift that held is traded.
Trend gate
Only trades drift agreeing with the 50-day SMA slope — backtests showed this alignment is the whole edge.

Implied volatility & options

IV
Implied Volatility — the market's expected future volatility baked into option prices. High IV = expensive options.
IV Rank
Where today's IV sits in its 1-year range (0=low, 100=high). The IV-rank strategy sells premium when high.
IV/HV ratio
Implied ÷ historical vol. >1 means options price in more move than the stock has shown (rich).
IV crush
The collapse of IV right after a known event (e.g. earnings). Why buying long premium into earnings loses — and why Strategy 1 was retired.
DTE / 0-DTE
Days To Expiration. 0-DTE expires the same day (max gamma, max theta decay).
ATM
At-The-Money — strike ≈ the current stock price.
Greeks (Δ Γ Θ V)
Sensitivity to price (delta), to delta (gamma), to time decay (theta — the daily bleed), and to IV (vega).
Iron condor / spread
Defined-risk option structures the IV-rank strategy sells (condor / bull-put spread) or buys (straddle).

Exits, risk & portfolio

TP / SL
Take-Profit / Stop-Loss on the option's value. HFT uses asymmetric +100% / −20% — let winners run, cut losers fast.
Trailing stop
A stop that ratchets up as the trade gains, locking in profit.
Scale-out
Closing part of a position at a target while letting the rest run.
Day vs Swing
≤1-DTE = day trade; multi-day hold = swing. They get separate position budgets.
Correlation cluster
A group of correlated tickers (megacap-growth, semis, index…). The bot caps how many positions stack into one cluster.
Drawdown governor
De-risks (half→quarter size) then halts new entries as equity falls from its high-water mark; plus a rolling weekly loss limit.
High-water mark
The peak equity reached; drawdown is measured from it.

Data sources

Tradier
Broker API — account, positions, orders, quotes, option chains (with Greeks), intraday timesales. Sandbox = paper.
yfinance
Free historical price data (daily + intraday) and earnings dates — used by backtests and as a fallback.
Google News (RSS)
Per-ticker headline feed (News tab + news-catalyst scoring).
SEC EDGAR (8-K)
Official material-event filings — the most authoritative catalyst source.
Stocktwits
Retail sentiment with explicit bull/bear tags (Social tab).
Reddit
Mention volume + keyword sentiment for retail buzz (Social tab).
Wikipedia
S&P 500 constituent list for universe building.
Unusual Whales (optional)
Options-flow data if an API key is configured; otherwise flow signals are skipped.
